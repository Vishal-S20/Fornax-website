Environmental Hazards

The Cost of Non-Recycling

Heavy Metal Leaching

If left in landfills, solar panels release Lead (Pb) and Cadmium (Cd). These toxic metals can seep into the soil and contaminate groundwater sources forever.

Micro-Glass Pollution

Unprocessed solar glass shatters into microscopic shards that do not decompose. This creates "glass-dust" pollution that harms local ecosystems and soil quality.

Resource Scarcity

Discarding panels wastes Silver and Silicon. Non-recycling forces aggressive new mining, leading to deforestation and massive carbon emissions.
